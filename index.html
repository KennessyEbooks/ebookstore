About The Author

Kenneth Chiakwa, BSc, MSc - PhD Candidate

Agricultural Economist | Educationist | Poultry Entrepreneur

Kenneth Chiakwa is an Agricultural Economist and a seasoned Secondary School Teacher with over 15 years of experience in classroom instruction and curriculum development. He holds a BSc and MSc in Agricultural Economics from the Enugu State University of Science and Technology, ESUT, and is currently a PhD Candidate.

As the CEO of Kennessy Poultry Services located at 23 Nwanmadu Street, Ugbene 2, Abakpa Nike, Enugu, he combines academic knowledge with practical, on-the-farm experience in both broiler and layers production.

Through Kennessy Ebooks Store, he is committed to bridging the knowledge gap by providing practical, affordable, and easy-to-implement guides for farmers, students, and teachers across Africa.
